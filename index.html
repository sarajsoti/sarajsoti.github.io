Hungary is a cosmopolitan country located in the heart of Europe, with its own vibrant culture nestled among other colorful nations. Explore the rich heritage that is preserved in this small country.

History

Hungary has more than a 1,000 year history, since the arrival of the seven chieftans in the Carpathian Basin in 896.

  • King Stephen
  • Ottoman Oppression

The Hungarian people have unique holiday traditions. There are some things they celebrate that those in the U.S. do not.

  1. Name Days
  2. St. Nicholas Day

  4. Easter Sprinkling
